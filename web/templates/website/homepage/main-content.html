One hundred and sixty years ago, divine cataclysms drove humanity into five sacred cities. Since The Herding, Korvessa has endured beneath the watchful eye of the Watcher—isolated, protected, bound by law and faith. The boundaries beyond the city remain deadly; none who venture beyond and attempt to escape return alive.

For sixty years, the Watcher's hooded emissaries have delivered rare gifts and sacred texts through the Sprinkling—a yearly rite that anchors the city's survival and belief. But five years ago, the Sprinkling did not come. Now, as faith fractures and factions vie for influence, whispers speak of another Scholar's Passage—a once-a-decade journey beyond the walls, guided by the Watcher's unseen hand.

What will you become in this walled world?

The Three Paths

Veloran - The Path of Order

Followers of Velora seek virtue, sacrifice, and the perfection of self through discipline. Knights, healers, and artisans find meaning in service and endurance. The scale balances all, and through steadfast duty comes strength.

Feyliksian - The Path of Chance

Devotees of Feyliks embrace fortune, trickery, and freedom. Gamblers, performers, and those who dance with risk find ecstasy in the unknown. The dice fall as they will—seize the moment, for fate is yours to claim.

Regalist - The Path of Dominion

Regalus demands ambition, strength, and the will to command. Lords, warriors, and those who hunger for power seek to bend the world to their vision. Authority flows to those with the will to seize it.
