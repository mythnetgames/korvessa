|cDoors, Locks, and Keypads (Builder+)|n

This helpfile describes how to use doors, locks, and combination keypad locks for exits.

|wAttaching Components|n
- |cattachdoor <direction>|n: Attach a door to an exit.
- |cattachlock <direction>|n: Attach a lock to a door on an exit.
- |cattachkeypad <direction>|n: Attach a keypad lock to a door on an exit.
- |cbulkattach <component> <direction1> [direction2 ...]|n: Bulk attach doors/locks/keypads to multiple exits.

|wProgramming Keypads|n
- |cprogramkeypad <direction> <combo>|n: Set the keypad's 8-digit combination (default: 00000000).
- |cshowcombo <direction>|n: Show the keypad combo (builder+ only).
- |centercombo <direction> <combo>|n: Enter a combo to unlock a keypad lock.
- Keypads lock out after 3 failed attempts; builder+ must reset.

|wRemoving Components|n
- |cremovedoor <direction>|n: Remove a door from an exit.
- |cremovelock <direction>|n: Remove a lock from a door on an exit.
- |cremovekeypad <direction>|n: Remove a keypad lock from a door on an exit.
- |cbulkremove <component> <direction1> [direction2 ...]|n: Bulk remove doors/locks/keypads from multiple exits.

|wDoor State Commands|n
- |copendoor <direction>|n: Open a door on an exit.
- |cclosedoor <direction>|n: Close a door on an exit.
- |clockdoor <direction> [key]|n: Lock a door (requires key).
- |cunlockdoor <direction> [key]|n: Unlock a door (requires key).

|wStatus and Customization|n
- |cdoorstatus <direction>|n: Show status of door, lock, and keypad (builder+ only).
- |csetdoormsg <direction> <type> <message>|n: Set custom open/close/lock/unlock messages for a door (builder+ only).

|wUsage Notes|n
- Only builder+ can attach/remove doors, locks, and keypads, or view/program keypad combos.
- Keypads are programmable to any 8-digit code; default is 00000000.
- All components can be attached/removed at any time by builder+.
- Future hooks exist for electronic hacking of keypads.
- Exits with closed/locked/keypad-locked doors will block movement until unlocked/opened.
- All attach/remove/program actions are logged to the BuilderAudit channel.
- Bulk operations allow fast management of multiple exits.
- Key objects must match lock key_id to unlock doors.
- Keypads lock out after 3 failed attempts; builder+ must reset.

|wExample|n
  attachdoor east
  attachlock east
  attachkeypad east
  programkeypad east 12345678
  showcombo east
  entercombo east 12345678
  opendoor east
  closedoor east
  lockdoor east mykey
  unlockdoor east mykey
  doorstatus east
  setdoormsg east open "The heavy door swings open with a creak."
  bulkattach door north south east
  bulkremove lock north south east
  removedoor east
  removelock east
  removekeypad east
